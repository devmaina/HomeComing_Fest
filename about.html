HomeComing Fest

Originating in the heart of Muranga, Homecoming Festival is dedicated to bringing together families, friends, and neighbors. In a world where technological advances and other challenges can lead to the disintegration of family bonds, we aim to provide a platform that promotes unity and togetherness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an annual celebration that not only entertains but also strengthens the bonds within families and communities. Through a variety of activities and events, we strive to provide a space where people can reconnect, have fun, and build lasting memories.

Local Roots, Global Vision

While we started as a local initiative in Muranga, we have ambitious plans to franchise the Homecoming Festival idea to other countries and communities worldwide. Get Involved

Whether you're a local business, an individual, or part of a community group, we invite you to join us in making Homecoming Festival a success. Sponsorship opportunities, volunteer roles, and participation in the event are all ways you can contribute to this meaningful cause.
